## Exclusion Rules

Skip:

- Generic "AI will change everything" commentary
- Unsourced screenshots
- Rewritten mainstream news without original links
- Funding news with no product or market implication
- GitHub repos with no demo, weak README, or stale commits
- Papers with no clear user-facing implication
